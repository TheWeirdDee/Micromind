### Day 23: Escrow Cryptography
How do MicroMind Escrow Letters work?
1. Write your letter in the UI.
2. We generate an AES key and encrypt it client-side.
3. The ciphertext is sent to Supabase.
4. The AES key is escrowed.
5. On the release date, the backend decrypts and releases the letter.

Pure math, total trust. 🔐
